Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I select different weapons?

A: From the main menu, tap on the "Weapons" button. You can choose between Normal, Laser, and Shotgun weapons.

Q: How does the shield work?

A: The shield is available when using the Normal weapon. It can absorb up to three hits before breaking and will not regenerate between stages.

Q: What's the difference between weapon types?

A: Normal weapon fires double shots and provides a shield. Laser can penetrate through multiple enemies. Shotgun fires in a spread pattern to hit multiple enemies.

Q: How do I defeat boss enemies?

A: Boss enemies have more health than regular enemies. Continue hitting them until they're destroyed. Watch out for their special attack patterns!

Q: The game is crashing, what should I do?

A: Please ensure your device is running the latest iOS version. If problems persist, close other background apps and restart your device. If the issue continues, please contact us with details about your device model and iOS version.

Game Tips

Controls

Touch and drag to move your ship. Touch anywhere on the screen to fire your weapon.

Strategies

  • You can destroy enemy projectiles by shooting at them
  • Enemy projectiles require three hits to be destroyed
  • The shield (Normal weapon only) can block enemy projectiles
  • Boss enemies have weak points - aim carefully!
