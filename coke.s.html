About Coke Tracker

Coke Tracker is a simple, easy-to-use application designed to help you monitor your cola consumption. Track both Regular Coke and Coke Zero consumption, view your history, and see your consumption statistics at a glance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new entry?

To add a new entry:

  1. Tap the "+" button in the top right corner
  2. Select the type of coke (Regular or Zero)
  3. Choose or input the amount consumed
  4. Tap "Add Entry" to save

How do I delete an entry?

To delete an entry, simply swipe left on the entry in the main list and tap "Delete".

Is my data backed up?

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled for your device, your app data will be included in that backup. For best results, ensure your device is regularly backed up.

Does this app track any data about me?

Coke Tracker is designed with privacy in mind. The app only stores the data you manually enter about your consumption. No personal information is collected, and all data remains on your device. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I export my data?

Currently, Coke Tracker does not include an export feature. This functionality is planned for a future update.

The app crashed, what should I do?

If you experience a crash:

  1. Ensure your iOS is updated to the latest version
  2. Close all background apps and restart Coke Tracker
  3. If problems persist, try deleting and reinstalling the app (note: this will delete your saved data)
  4. Contact our support email with details about the crash

Troubleshooting

App Not Saving Data

If your entries are not being saved between sessions:

  • Ensure you have sufficient device storage
  • Check that you're not in low power mode, which can affect background processes
  • Make sure you've updated to the latest version of the app

Display Issues

If text or UI elements appear incorrectly:

  • Check your device's display settings
  • Try toggling between light and dark mode in your iOS settings
  • Restart the app
