Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 16, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Coke Tracker. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our application. Coke Tracker is designed to be simple and privacy-focused, collecting only the minimum information required to provide our service.

Developed by: MOTOKI FUJIWARA

Information We Collect

Coke Tracker collects and stores the following information:

  • User-provided tracking data: This includes the date, type (Regular or Zero), and amount of coke you consume as entered by you in the application.
  • Device storage information: The app stores your consumption data locally on your device using Apple's secure UserDefaults system. This information does not leave your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Usage statistics
  • Device information
  • Any other data not explicitly mentioned above

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Display your consumption history and statistics within the app
  • Save your records between app sessions

All processing occurs locally on your device. We do not transmit, share, or analyze your data remotely.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your data with any third parties. Your information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any other external services.

The only potential for data sharing occurs if:

  • You explicitly choose to export or share your data using iOS share features
  • You back up your device to iCloud or iTunes, in which case your app data may be included in that backup according to your device settings

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Since all data is stored locally on your device, it benefits from the built-in security features of iOS, including sandboxing and device encryption if enabled.

Your Rights and Control

You maintain complete control over your data:

  • You can view all stored data directly within the app
  • You can delete individual entries or all entries at any time
  • You can completely remove the app, which will delete all associated data (unless backed up separately)

Children's Privacy

Our application does not collect personal information and is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date, and significant changes will be communicated via an app update notice.
